Support - Arrow Dash

Need help with Arrow Dash? We're here to help you with gameplay issues, bugs, feedback, or suggestions.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I control the arrow?

Tap and hold the screen to control the movement. Timing and rhythm are key to surviving longer.

Is Arrow Dash an offline game?

Yes. Arrow Dash can be played completely offline.

Why is the game difficult?

Arrow Dash is designed to be challenging and skill-based, inspired by fast reflex arcade games.

The game feels laggy or crashes. What should I do?

Make sure your device is updated to the latest iOS version. Restarting the app or device may also help resolve performance issues.

Report a Bug or Issue

If you encounter bugs, crashes, or gameplay issues, please email us with your device model and a brief description of the problem.
