Frequently Asked Questions

How do I colour a template?

Pick a colour from the wheel at the bottom of the screen, then tap any region of the artwork to fill it. Each fill animates in instantly.

Can I undo a fill?

Yes. Use the undo and redo arrows in the toolbar above the palette to step backward or forward through your recent fills.

Does my progress save automatically?

Yes. Colourify saves your progress on your device after every fill, so you can close the app and pick up exactly where you left off.

Do I have to fill every region?

No. You're free to leave any region white or unfilled if that's the look you want — tap the checkmark in the toolbar when you consider a piece finished.

How do I save or share my finished artwork?

Tap the save icon in the toolbar. You'll be asked for permission to add the image to your Photos library, after which you can also share it directly from the share sheet.

What are colouring packs?

Packs are bundles of additional colouring templates. Some packs are free, and others can be unlocked with a one-time in-app purchase from the More Packs tab.

I reinstalled the app — can I get my purchased packs back?

Yes. Purchased packs are tied to your Apple ID. Look for a "Restore Purchases" option in the More Packs tab and sign in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Does Colourify work offline?

Yes, Colourify works fully offline. No account or internet connection is required to colour, save progress, or view your artwork.

Privacy

Colourify doesn't require an account and doesn't collect personal information. Your colouring progress is stored locally on your device. Saving artwork uses Photos "Add Only" access, used only to save the image you create.
