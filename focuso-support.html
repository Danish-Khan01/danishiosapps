Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a focus session?

Open the Timer tab, select your mode (Focus, Short Break, or Long Break), then tap the play button. The default focus duration is 25 minutes.

Can I change the timer duration?

Yes! Go to Settings → Timer and adjust Focus, Short Break, and Long Break durations to fit your workflow.

What are ambient sounds?

Ambient sounds are background audio loops (rain, forest, café, ocean, fire, white noise) designed to help you concentrate. Tap the Ambient card on the Timer screen to choose and toggle sounds.

How does the streak work?

Your streak counts consecutive days you've completed at least one focus session. Check your current streak on the Stats tab.

Why am I seeing a paywall?

Focuso Pro unlocks streak tracking, detailed stats, ambient sounds, and custom intervals. You can start with a 3-day free trial or choose a monthly or yearly plan. All your data is preserved if you subscribe later.

Can I restore my purchase on a new device?

Yes. On the paywall or in Settings, tap "Restore Purchases" and sign in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

This Privacy Policy explains how Danish Khan operates the Focuso iOS application ("App") and how information may be handled when you use it.

Focuso does not require account creation and does not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or passwords through the App.

2. Local Device Storage

Your session history, streak data, timer settings, and app preferences are saved locally on your iPhone or iPad to provide core app functionality.
