Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 27, 2026

Stoic Reflection (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Stoic Reflection mobile application (“App”).

1. Information We Collect

Stoic Reflection does not directly collect, store, or share personal information from users.

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Precise location
  • Contacts
  • Photos
  • Messages
  • Journal reflections (unless stored locally on your device)

2. Local Device Storage

Some app data such as streaks, reminders, progress, settings, and reflections may be stored locally on your device to provide app functionality. This data is not transmitted to us.

3. Third-Party Advertising (Google AdMob)

Stoic Reflection may display advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and process certain data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads, measure performance, and prevent fraud.

  • Device identifiers
  • Approximate location
  • Advertising data
  • Usage data

4. Data Security

Since Stoic Reflection does not directly collect personal data, we do not maintain external databases of user personal information. Any data stored locally on your device is protected by your device's own security settings.

5. Children's Privacy

Stoic Reflection is not specifically directed to children under 13. If required by law, ads shown through AdMob may be subject to age-related protections.

6. Your Choices

  • Reset advertising ID
  • Limit ad tracking (where available)
  • Manage consent settings in supported regions

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted within the app or on the app listing page.
