Privacy Policy

VocabZ: Learn Common Words

Effective Date: May 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Danish Khan operates the VocabZ: Learn Common Words iOS application ("App") and how information may be handled when you use it.

1. Information We Collect

VocabZ does not require account creation and does not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or passwords through the App.

The App stores your learning progress, saved words, collections, and preferences locally on your device.

2. Local Device Storage

Your vocabulary collections, progress data, learned words, and settings are saved locally on your iPhone or iPad to provide core app functionality.

This data is under your device control and may be removed by deleting the App.

3. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services that collect limited information automatically.

  • Google AdMob – used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, usage data, and ad interaction data in accordance with Google's policies.
  • Apple Analytics – Apple may provide aggregated analytics, diagnostics, or crash information to help improve app performance.

Please review the privacy policies of these providers for more details.

4. How Information Is Used

  • Provide vocabulary learning features
  • Save your progress and collections locally
  • Show ads through AdMob
  • Improve stability, reliability, and performance

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not directly share personal information because the App does not collect personal account data. Third-party providers may process limited technical data as described above.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and is not specifically directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted personal data through a third-party service, please contact the relevant provider.

7. Security

We aim to use reasonable measures to protect the App experience. However, no storage or transmission method is completely secure.

8. Your Choices

  • You may delete locally stored app data by removing the App from your device.
  • You may manage ad personalization and privacy permissions in your iOS settings where available.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes become effective when the updated version is posted.
