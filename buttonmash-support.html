Frequently Asked Questions

The sound isn't working. What should I do?

Check if your device is in Silent Mode (Mute switch on). The game respects system settings. Also, make sure your volume is turned up — FinBall uses the playback audio session.

Can I play online against other people?

Currently, FinBall is a local multiplayer game (same device) only. Both players tap on the same screen to compete. We are exploring online features for future updates!

Does the game support haptic feedback?

Yes! If you are using an iPhone 7 or newer, every tap will provide tactile feedback to make the football action feel more real. Haptics are disabled in Simulator builds.

How do I score goals?

Tap faster than your opponent on your side of the screen to push the ball toward their goal. First to 3 goals wins the match!

Can I play solo?

FinBall is designed for 2 players on the same device. Grab a friend, family member, or rival and battle it out!
