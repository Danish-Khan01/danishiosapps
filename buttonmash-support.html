Frequently Asked Questions

The sound isn't working. What should I do?

Check if your device is in Silent Mode (Mute switch on). The game respects system settings. Also, check the in-game mute toggle on the main menu.

Can I play online against other people?

Currently, Button Mash is a local multiplayer game (same device) only. We are exploring online features for future updates!

Does the game support haptic feedback?

Yes! If you are using an iPhone 7 or newer, every tap will provide tactile feedback to make the competition feel more real.
