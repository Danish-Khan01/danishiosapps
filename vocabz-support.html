Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my progress?
Your progress, saved words, and collections are stored locally on your device unless cloud sync is added in future updates.

Can I use the app offline?
Yes, most core learning features work offline.

How do I save words?
Use the collection feature to save words and review them anytime.
