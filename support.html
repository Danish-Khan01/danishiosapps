Frequently Asked Questions

Game progress lost?
Progress is stored locally. If the app is removed without a backup, progress may not be recoverable.

Game not responding?
Restart the app, update to the latest version, or restart your device.

Ads not loading?
Check your internet connection and try again later.

Found a bug?
Email us with your device model, iOS version, and a description of the issue.
