Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 25, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the FinBall mobile application (herein referred to as "the App") for mobile devices. This service is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is.

1. Information Collection and Use

The App does not require you to create an account or provide personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number to play.

However, the App may automatically collect certain information, including but not limited to:

  • Device type and unique device identifiers
  • Operating system version
  • Game statistics and match history (stored locally on device)
  • Crash reports and performance data

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These may include:

  • Google AdMob: Used for displaying interstitial advertisements. AdMob may use device identifiers to show personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings.
  • Apple StoreKit: Used for App Store review prompts (SKStoreReviewController). No personal data is collected.

3. Local Storage

The App stores your game settings, player profiles, match history, and head-to-head statistics locally on your device using UserDefaults. This data is not transmitted to our servers. Player names entered during gameplay are stored locally and are not shared with any third party.

4. Advertising

The App displays interstitial advertisements provided by Google AdMob. These ads may be personalized based on your device identifier. You can reset your advertising identifier or opt out of personalized ads in your device Settings > Privacy > Advertising.

5. Children's Privacy

This App is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take necessary actions.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

All game data is stored locally on your device. To delete all game data, simply uninstall the App from your device. There is no cloud backup or server-side data to delete.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Any changes are effective immediately upon posting to this page.
